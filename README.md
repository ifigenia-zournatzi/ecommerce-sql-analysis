# 📊 E-commerce Profitability & Discount Strategy Analysis

### 🎯 Project Overview
In this project, I acted as a Data Analyst for a retail company, analyzing a dataset of nearly 10,000 transactions. The goal was to identify which categories drive profit, which areas cause financial bleeding, and how the company's discount strategy impacts the bottom line.

### 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
* **SQL Dialect:** SQLite
* **Environment:** DBeaver
* **Techniques Used:** Aggregations, Data Segmentation (`CASE WHEN`), Common Table Expressions (CTEs).

### 💡 Key Business Insights
1. **The Core Performers:** Technology is the most profitable category, while California is the dominant state in terms of sales volume and profit.
2. **The "Bleeding" Products:** The 'Tables' sub-category is causing significant financial losses.
3. **The Discount Trap:** A root-cause analysis revealed that discounts of 30% or higher consistently lead to negative profit margins. 

### 🚀 Strategic Recommendations
* **Revise Discount Policy:** Immediately cap discounts at 20-25% for high-volume items to stop profit leakage.
* **Investigate 'Tables':** Review the supply chain or pricing model for Tables, as they are currently unprofitable even at low discount rates.
